About Me

Family. Integrity. Balance. These are my most fundamental values that I try to live day in and day out.

Let me start with family. I am the oldest of five children born to Edward and Paula Blakeley. My dad is from the small, remote island of Guam in Micronesia. My mother is of Spanish descent, and was born and raised here in the bay area. My family has made a huge impact on the person that I am today. I want nothing more than to make my parents proud, and set a good example for my siblings.

Integrity is being true in ones dealings with others, and also with oneself. I strive to act with absolute integrity because it is the one factor that has the biggest impact on my happiness.

Balance and renewal are integral parts of the culmination of a complete individual. In my life I stive to balance work and achievement with reflection and relationship building. I also really enjoy meditating which I feel helps to achieve balance.

My Work

Currently, I am working in fine dining. I have a solid knowledge of food and wine that I have acquired working in some of the best restaurants in the world. I also own a business, Professional Automotive, which specializes in vehicle repair, restoration, and sales. Finally I am a student at UC Berkeley studying Economics. I think that this is an appropriate discipline that really plays to my strengths in quantative reasoning and logical analysis of decesion making. I look forward to obtaining my degree and doing something that I really care about. Eventually I would like to start another business that deals with food and beverage service.
